Nitecore MT06MD Torch

A tool to check your dazzle and PLR reflexes. More potent and compact than your ophthalmoscope

I often find that vets struggle to judge Dazzle and PLRs. These are cornerstones of your neuro-ophthalmology exam, which frequently cannot be determined with most ophthalmoscopes. Why? They are not potent enough.

A This pen torch like this can solve that problem for very little money.

At its lowest setting, it potentially can allow you retinal examination with condensing lens without causing undue discomfort
